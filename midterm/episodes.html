Episode

Season 1

1. Pilot

Rory enters a prestigious school, and Lorelai has no choice but to swallow her pride and ask her parents for help.

2. Lorelai's first day at Chilton

Rory's first day at Chilton is a nightmare: Lorelai's outfit is inappropriate for the occasion, and Rory meets some insufferable classmates.

3. Family jealousy

When Rory and Richard bond over a round of golf, Lorelai grows jealous of her daughter's deep connection with her grandparents.

4. Deer hunting

Rory gets a bad grade at school, so Lorelai helps her with an important exam on Shakespeare, but disaster strikes on the day of the test.

5. Cinnamon's funeral

Lorelai admits to having feelings for someone. Meanwhile, Rory is in a relationship that is slowly progressing.

6. Rory's birthday

Rory confronts her grandmother when she wants to throw her a formal birthday party. Meanwhile, Lorelai organizes a relaxed get-together to celebrate.

7. The first kiss

Rory has a romantic moment with Dean, but hides it from Lorelai. When she finds out from someone else, she invites Dean over to her house to watch a movie.

8. Love and snow

Rory is snowed in at her grandparents' house, and Lorelai spends the day with her boyfriend. Meanwhile, Lane misses Rory and goes to visit her.

9. Rory's dance

Rory attends her first dance with Dean and comes home late, causing Emily to argue with Lorelai about her daughter's parenting skills.

10. Knowing how to forgive

An accident occurs while the Gilmore family is celebrating Christmas. Meanwhile, Dean assures Lorelai that nothing happened the night he fell asleep with Rory.

11. Divorce and kisses

Paris causes a scandal by exposing Lorelai's relationship with a teacher, and Rory confronts her.

12. Double date

Lorelai goes on a hellish double date with Sookie, Jackson, and his weird cousin. Meanwhile, Rory goes on a couple date with Lane, Dean, and a friend of his.
